Optimize Fertility

When the path to your baby feels uncertain, we create a personalized plan to help optimize your fertility.

Optimize Fertility is a personalized partnership designed to help you optimize your fertility — using evidence-based nutrition thoughtfully tailored to you.

Schedule Call Enroll in Optimize Fertility

What This Partnership Includes

  • Comprehensive assessment of your fertility history, laboratory data, symptoms, and goals
  • A personalized nutrition strategy designed to optimize fertility and support your individual goals
  • Weekly 1:1 sessions to review progress and adjust priorities
  • Ongoing messaging support between sessions
  • Strategic guidance aligned with your cycle and treatment plan

Why This Work Takes Time

Fertility develops over months — not days. Egg and sperm development, hormone signaling, and nutrient status evolve gradually.

Optimize Fertility is structured as a focused three-month partnership aligned with the timeline of egg and sperm development — designed to support measurable, meaningful progress.

Who This Is For

  • Trying to conceive and unsure where to focus
  • Preparing for or between IVF and fertility treatments
  • Wanting to support egg, sperm, and cycle health
  • Navigating PCOS, endometriosis, irregular cycles, or insulin resistance
  • Experiencing unexplained infertility
  • Coming off birth control and wanting to support cycle regulation
  • Looking for guidance without restrictive dieting or fear-based messaging

Structured Weekly Support

  • Weekly 1:1 meetings to review progress, labs, symptoms, and priorities
  • Messaging between sessions for timely questions and adjustments
  • A clear, evolving plan that reflects where you are in your cycle or treatment

Optimize Fertility begins with a focused three-month partnership. Many clients find this structured period provides the clarity and direction they need — and some choose to continue beyond this time based on what feels most supportive for their journey.
