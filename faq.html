o A fertility dietitian is a Registered Dietitian who specializes in nutrition therapy to support individuals and couples in optimizing their fertility and reproductive health.

o Nutrition can impact hormonal balance, ovulation, sperm quality, egg quality and overall reproductive health. Proper nutrition can help improve egg and sperm quality. It can address nutrient deficiencies and improve overall health. Good nutrition can increase the chance of conception, healthy baby and improve the health of future generations.

o The first consultation typically involves a comprehensive assessment of your medical history, fertility journey, dietary habits, lifestyle, and fertility goals. You will work with your dietitian to develop a personalized and tailored plan to help you meet your goals.

o Yes, a referral is required to see a Fertility Dietitian. A referral helps your dietitian communicate with your healthcare team to ensure you receive optimal care.
