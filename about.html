We help people actualize their dreams of parenthood.

Lorena Akerman, MS, RD, CDN, CDCES

Hi, I’m Lorena — a Registered Dietitian with over 15 years of experience, and I specialize in using evidence-based nutrition to support fertility. This work grew from my own longing to become a mother and is now driven by the privilege of helping others build the families of their dreams.

Nutrition is one of the most powerful (yet underutilized) tools in reproductive health. Rooted in science and clinical evidence, nutrition can directly influence hormone balance, egg and sperm quality, and even implantation. For many, it’s the missing link in an otherwise exhaustive fertility journey.

I help individuals and couples use targeted nutrition strategies to address both explained and unexplained infertility — whether you're trying naturally or navigating IUI, IVF, or other assisted treatments. Wherever you are in your journey, you're in the right place. My goal is to nourish your body, support your path, and help increase your chances of parenthood.
