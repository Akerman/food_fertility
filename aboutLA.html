Lorena Akerman, MS, RD, CDN, CDCES

Lorena Akerman, MS, RD, CDN, CDCES specializes in fertility nutrition, nutrition in pregnancy and diabetes. Her dedication to family and well-being inspired her to delve deeply into the relationship between nutrition, fertility, and the health of future generations.

Driven by a passion for family and a commitment to client care, Lorena founded Food and Fertility. Her goal is to empower couples and individuals to make lifestyle modifications to increase fertility and help them actualize their dreams of parenthood.
